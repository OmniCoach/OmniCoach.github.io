About OmniCoach

You, Your Teams, & Your Community

Forget about the pain of creating and distributing your workouts by paper. Easily and quickly create custom workouts and assign them to hundreds of athletes with the touch of a button.

With almost 600 exercises in our database, we're almost certain to have your fitness plans covered. If something's missing, relax. Create custom exercises to satisfy your every training need.

Keep an eye on your teams and athletes progress with our built in exercise tracking. Identify trends, see what works and what doesn't.

How It Works

STEP 1

Sign up for a school trial, and have your athletes sign up. We'll set up everything you need, no need to lift a finger.

STEP 2

Select or quickly create a workout, select a team, and take a breather. You're done.

STEP 3

Watch as your athletes pull up your workouts on their phone or computer, and enter their data straight into your account.

STEP 4

Click on any team or athlete to get an in depth progress analysis. Realize this took less than 20 minutes. Consider taking up a hobby.
